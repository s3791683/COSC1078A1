Project Idea

Overview

The project idea which I have a chosen to undertake will involve the development of a smartphone application. The app will be specifically designed for individuals who want to remember key information from certain areas of study. In which they will be able to create questions and answers to choose from in a test-like format to test their knowledge and too see if they have retained their desired information. The app will incorporate a range of incentives such as a point system to show the individual of their progress, and a range of colourful and inviting visuals to make the app more exciting and favourable above others.

Motivation

Students are always faced with the task of memorizing key information for content heavy subjects. To ease the pressure and stress of retaining large amounts of information I have proposed the creation of an app that will help to not only remember the preferred information but to make it a fun and easy experience. Students are always faced with the idea of learning information from a 1000 paged textbook, now students will be able to choose what information is necessary to be learnt, which can save time and sleep. Furthermore, regardless of age and complexity of information this app will be purposeful for tertiary, secondary and primary students making it accessible for the wider communities across Australia and the world.

Description

The Apps features will include a language translator which will allow individuals from other nationalities to also use the app the way it is intended to. This language translator will be a feature in the settings tab which will be accompanied by the countries Flag if applicable. In addition, the app will include a local database system in which the user can store and access past tests and view their past marks which will indicate to the user their overall improvement. The app will comprise of a start-up menu which will give the user the ability to create/edit tests, access their results and undergo their desired tests. The create/edit tests feature will allow the user to give the Test a “Title” and a desired number of questions and answers. If the user feels they no longer require a specific question they can delete it by accessing the create/edit tests page. The results page will incorporate 5 main features, the percentage from the test, the time in which the user took to complete the test, the users past test results (if any), the points gained from completing the test and the overall grade from that test. The overall grade will determine how many points are awarded to the individual and the amount of points gained will determine progress of the individuals rank. The ranks will be titled with fun names such as rank 1 – Purple Primary Pupil, rank 2 – Slimy Secondary Student and rank 3 – Tired Tertiary Tutor etc. The ranks and their titles can be viewed on the home screen of the App, as well as a small progress bar to indicate the points needed to achieve the next rank. As the user increases with rank it will take more points for the user to reach the next rank. Hence, achieving rank 1 will be easier than achieving rank 15. Once the user wishes to begin the desired test, they will be accompanied by a begin test button which will initiate the timer and a metronome melody, this timer feature will be visible in the bottom right of the app when undergoing a test. Both the timer and melody feature can be deactivated in the settings tab on the front screen, as some users may feel pressured from the sound and visual representation of time loss. Furthermore, the finish test button will automatically take the user to the results page. After the user is satisfied with their results the user will be sent to the front screen which will show their rank and progress bar being updated through a short and exciting animation. The animation will incorporate a visual representation of the individual rank names for example rank 2 (slimy secondary student) will depict a secondary student covered in green gooey slime, rank 3(Tired Tertiary Tutor) will elucidate a tertiary tutor who is sleeping on a book etc. This will give the user an incentive to see all the animations of all the different and unique ranks which can be achieved through the completion of their own studies.
